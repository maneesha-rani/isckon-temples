History of ISKCON

ISKCON was founded by AC Bhaktivedanta Swami in 1966 in New York City to promote the practice of Bhakti Yoga or Krishna Consciousness as a means to enlightenment. ISKCON devotes itself to the worship of Lord Krishna, whom they believe is the Supreme Being.
